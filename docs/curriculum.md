# R182 Commander - Curriculum Framework

# Curriculum Philosophy

The curriculum is designed to develop aircraft command competence.

The goal is not to memorise the POH.

The goal is to understand:

- The aircraft
- Its systems
- Its limitations
- Its performance
- Its failure modes
- The decisions required to operate it safely

The curriculum follows four stages:

```text
Knowledge
Understanding
Application
Command
```

Every lesson should support progression through these stages.

---

# Competency Progression

Every mission contributes to:

- Aircraft Systems
- Configuration Management
- Fuel Management
- Performance Planning
- Emergency Management
- Threat Recognition
- Decision Making
- Aircraft Command

Competency levels:

```text
0 - Unseen

1 - Recognise

2 - Explain

3 - Apply

4 - Command
```

The objective of the curriculum is to achieve Level 4 competency across all critical aircraft systems and emergency management domains.
